A Canterbury Favourite Since Day One

Located on the charming St Peter's Street in the heart of Canterbury, My Cafe has been serving locals and visitors alike with hearty, honest British fare. We believe in generous portions, fair prices, and a warm welcome for everyone who walks through our door.

Whether you're after a full English to start your day, a comforting lunch, or just a cup of tea and a chat, we're here seven days a week.
